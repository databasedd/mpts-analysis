Tribunal hearings and decisions

We run tribunal hearings for doctors, physician associates (PAs) and anaesthesia associates (AAs). They hear evidence and decide whether a doctor, PA or AA is fit to practise in the UK and whether their registration should be restricted. See how these hearings work.

Medical Practitioners Tribunal Service

We are the tribunal service for doctors, physician associates (PAs) and anaesthesia associates (AAs). We run hearings that make independent decisions about whether doctors, PAs and AAs are fit to practise in the UK.
